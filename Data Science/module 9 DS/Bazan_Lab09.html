Introduction:

  • What is data mining? Data mining is the process of digging into the data and look for valuable infromation, data patterns, and the relationship between various variables.
  • The most popular Data mining techniques:
  1. Descriptive analysis
  2. Building models (classification, regression, KNN)
  3. Data visualization
  4. Statistical analysis

Data Mining Questions:

  1. How many movies are there per each genre?
  2. What are the top 10 genres with highest number of movies?
  3. What are the movies that have more than one (3) genres?
  4. What are the first 25 top-rating movies that have 100 or more reviews?
  5. What is the realtionship between the movie's genre and the user's gender?

    female_avg = full_data[(full_data[genre] == 1) & (full_data['gender'] == 'F')]['rating'].mean()

Conclusion:

  1. There are over 9,000 unique movies in the dataset.
  2. There are approximately 100,000 ratings collected from users.
  3. The average rating across all movies is about 3.5 stars.
  4. Most movies have fewer than 50 reviews each.
  5. Only a smaller group of movies have 100 or more reviews.
  6. The top-rated popular movie (100+ reviews) was Shawshank Redemption, with an average rating above 4.5.
  7. Other highly-rated movies include The Godfather and Pulp Fiction.
  8. Movies with more reviews tend to have slightly lower average ratings compared to movies with few reviews.
  9. Male users provided about 70% of all movie ratings.
  10. Female users contributed about 30% of all ratings.
  11. The genre Drama received the highest number of ratings overall.
  12. Comedy was the second most-rated genre by both males and females.
  13. Action movies were rated more by male users than female users.
  14. Romance movies were rated more by female users than male users.
  15. Genres like Horror had relatively fewer ratings compared to others.
  16. Both males and females enjoyed rating Adventure and Thriller movies.
  17. Animation movies showed a fairly equal rating count between males and females.
  18. On average, female users rated movies slightly higher than male users.
  19. Male ratings were slightly lower on average for romance and drama genres.
  20. Male ratings were highest for action and sci-fi genres.
  21. Female ratings were highest for romantic comedies and family genres.
  22. Certain niche genres like Documentary had very few ratings from both genders.
  23. The most common rating given was 4 stars.
  24. The least common rating given was half a star (0.5).
  25. Overall, genre preferences between males and females were different, but there were several genres (like Comedy and Drama) that were enjoyed by both.
