Introduction:

What is data visualization (VIZ)?

  • Graphical presentation of data.

What are the benefits of data visualization (VIZ)?

  1. Helps with discovering the data patterns
  2. Helps with more effective communication
  3. Helps non-technical people to understand the data better or easier
  4. Helps with understanding the data distribution
  5. Helps with discovering the current trends in the dataset

What are the types of data visualization? There are 3 types of data visulaization.

  1. Univariate Visualization: plotting one variable.
  2. Bivariate Visualization: plotting two variables.
  3. Multivariate Visualization: plotting more than two variables.

What are popular types of data visualization plots (Viz) tools/techniques?

  1. Line Plots
  2. Bar Plots / Box chart
  3. Pie Charts
  4. Scatter Plots
  5. Histograms
  6. Heatmaps

What are the popular libraries used for data visualization(Viz)?

  1. matplotlib.pyplot
  2. seaborn

Heatmaps:

  1. Heatmaps are seaborn library plot that displays correlation
  2. What is correlation? Correlation is the relationship between variables
  3. Correlation can be positive or negative
  4. If the variables move together in either directions (up or down), we call this a positive correlation.
  5. If the variables move in opposite directions(one goes up and the other goes down), we call this a negative correlation.
  6. A heatmap is designed for numeric values only.
  7. The range of correlation in the heatmap goes from -1 to +1.
